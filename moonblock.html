Enter a question and cast the moon blocks. The blocks land visually first; the result is then calculated from the visible faces.

How to read it

Holy Answer · One flat + one curved A positive answer. Traditionally read as yes, permitted, blessed, or suitable to proceed.
Laughing Answer · Two flat sides The question may be unclear, premature, or not ready to be answered. Rephrase and ask again later.
Negative Answer · Two curved sides A negative answer. Traditionally read as no, not approved, or not the right direction for now.

History

Smoothness principle: the animation uses a single requestAnimationFrame loop and GPU-friendly translate3d transforms. Accuracy principle: the app lands the visible blocks first, reads their data-face values, and only then prints the result.
