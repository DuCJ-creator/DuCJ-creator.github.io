<p>One September night a family had gathered round their hearth, and piled it high with the driftwood of mountain streams. The house was known to be situated in the Notch of the White Hills, where the wind goeth at its will. They dwelt in a cold spot and a dangerous one, right at the foot of a mountain, but they were a cheerful family and felt secure.</p>
<p>A stranger entered the cottage, a young man, with a pack on his back. He was a wayfarer, but the family received him warmly as if he were an old friend. The secret of the young man's character was a high and <span class="vocab-tip" data-tip="abstracted：理想化的、出世的">abstracted</span> ambition. He could have borne to live an undistinguished life, but not to die and leave no track behind him.</p>
<blockquote>
    "I cannot die without leaving some monument of my existence behind me!"<br>
    <span style="font-size:14px; font-style:normal; color:#8c8a88;">（我不能死而無名，我必須在世上留下我曾存在過的碑石！）</span>
</blockquote>
<p>The young man poured out his heart to the family. "I must have my name recorded on a page of history!" he cried. As he spoke, a terrible sound was heard from the mountain above. It was the roar of a landslide! The family and the guest shrieked and fled from the house into the night for safety. But the landslide rushed directly over the path where they ran, burying them forever, while the little wooden cottage stayed perfectly untouched, standing alone in the morning sun.</p>
