<p>Two roads <span class="vocab-tip" data-tip="diverged：分叉、分道揚鑣">diverged</span> in a yellow wood,<br>
And sorry I could not travel both<br>
And be one traveler, long I stood<br>
And look down one as far as I could<br>
To where it bent in the <span class="vocab-tip" data-tip="undergrowth：灌木叢、林下葉層">undergrowth</span>;</p>
<p>Then took the other, as just as fair,<br>
And having perhaps the better claim,<br>
Because it was grassy and wanted wear;<br>
Though as for that the passing there<br>
Had worn them really about the same,</p>
<blockquote>
    "Two roads diverged in a wood, and I—I took the one less traveled by, and that has made all the difference."<br>
    <span style="font-size:14px; font-style:normal; color:#8c8a88;">（黃樹林裡分叉出兩條路，而我——選擇了人跡罕至的那一條，這便決定了我一生的道路。）</span>
</blockquote>
<p>And both that morning equally lay<br>
In leaves no step had trodden black.<br>
Oh, I kept the first for another day!<br>
Yet knowing how way leads on to way,<br>
I doubted if I should ever come back.</p>
<p>I shall be telling this with a sigh<br>
Somewhere ages and ages hence:<br>
Two roads diverged in a wood, and I—<br>
I took the one less traveled by,<br>
And that has made all the <span class="vocab-tip" data-tip="difference：巨大的改變、差別">difference</span>.</p>
