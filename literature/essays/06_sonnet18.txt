<p>Shall I compare thee to a summer's day?<br>
Thou art more lovely and more <span class="vocab-tip" data-tip="temperate：溫和的、有節制的">temperate</span>:<br>
Rough winds do shake the darling buds of May,<br>
And summer's lease hath all too short a date:</p>
<p>Sometime too hot the eye of heaven shines,<br>
And often is his gold complexion dimm'd;<br>
And every fair from fair sometime declines,<br>
By chance or nature's changing course untrimm'm;</p>
<blockquote>
    "So long as men can breathe or eyes can see, so long lives this and this gives life to thee."<br>
    <span style="font-size:14px; font-style:normal; color:#8c8a88;"> breathe or eyes can see, so long lives this and this gives life to thee."<br>只要人類還能呼吸、眼睛還能看見，這首詩就會流傳下去，賦予你永恆的生命。）</span>
</blockquote>
<p>But thy eternal summer shall not fade<br>
Nor lose possession of that fair thou owest;<br>
Nor shall death brag thou wander'st in his shade,<br>
When in eternal lines to time thou growest:</p>
<p>So long as men can breathe or eyes can see,<br>
So long lives this and this gives life to thee.</p>
