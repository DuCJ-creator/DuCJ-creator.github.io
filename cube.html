How to Play

  • Desktop: Drag with mouse to rotate view • Use keyboard shortcuts for quick control
  • Mobile: Drag with finger to rotate view • Tap buttons to rotate layers
  • U/F/R/L/D/B buttons/keys rotate layers clockwise
  • Buttons/keys with ' rotate counterclockwise
  • Eye buttons/1,2,3,0 keys quickly change your view
  • Goal: Restore each face to a single color
